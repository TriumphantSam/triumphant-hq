You've probably heard the term "AI" thrown around everywhere. But there's a critical piece of the AI puzzle that doesn't get enough attention: the human element.

## What is Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)?

Human-in-the-Loop is a model where humans work alongside AI systems to:
- **Train** the AI with high-quality labeled data
- **Validate** the AI's outputs for accuracy
- **Correct** mistakes that the AI makes
- **Improve** the model over time

Think of it as quality control for artificial intelligence.

## Why Companies Need It

No AI model is perfect. ChatGPT sometimes "hallucinates." Image recognition systems sometimes misidentify objects. Self-driving cars still need human oversight.
